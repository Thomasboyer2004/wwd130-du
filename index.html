About CSSA

The Chinese Students and Scholars Association (CSSA) at BYUI is a student-led organization that promotes cultural exchange, provides academic and life support, and organizes social activities for Chinese students and scholars. Our mission is to create a welcoming community and bridge the gap between Chinese members and the university environment.
